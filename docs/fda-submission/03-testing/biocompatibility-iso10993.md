## 6. BIOLOGICAL SAFETY ASSESSMENT

### 6.1 Risk Analysis

| Hazard | Likelihood | Severity | Risk Level | Mitigation |
|--------|------------|----------|------------|------------|
| Skin sensitization | Low | Moderate | Acceptable | Hypoallergenic materials |
| Skin irritation | Low | Minor | Acceptable | Limited duration, site rotation |
| Allergic reaction | Rare | Moderate | Acceptable | Pre-use screening |
| Infection | Rare | Moderate | Acceptable | Single-use electrodes |

### 6.2 Clinical Experience

- Ag/AgCl electrodes used globally for >50 years
- No serious adverse events from electrode contact
- Expected events: mild skin redness (<5% incidence)
